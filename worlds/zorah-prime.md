A harsh desert world orbiting twin suns. Birthplace of the Zorathians.

## Geography

Endless dunes of orange sand stretch across the equatorial belt. The polar regions hold the only permanent water — frozen lakes beneath kilometers of rock.

## Climate

Surface temperatures exceed 50°C at midday. Settlements cluster in deep canyons where shade provides relief. Nights drop to -10°C as heat radiates into the thin atmosphere.

## History

The Zorathians evolved here over millions of years, their biology shaped by scarcity. When they developed spaceflight, they spread to seventeen colony worlds within two centuries — but Zorah Prime remains the spiritual center of their civilization.

## Notable Locations

- **The Rift of Voices** — A canyon system where wind creates haunting harmonics
- **Karath Spire** — Ancient monument predating recorded history
- **New Dawn Station** — The primary spaceport, built into a mesa
