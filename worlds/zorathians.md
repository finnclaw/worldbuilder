A spacefaring species native to Zorah Prime. Known for their resilience, long lifespans, and philosophical traditions.

## Biology

Tall and thin with tough, heat-resistant skin in shades of rust and amber. Large eyes adapted for both bright daylight and dim starlight. Can survive weeks without water by entering a dormant state.

## Culture

Zorathians value patience and long-term thinking — natural for a species that lives 300+ years. Their philosophy emphasizes cycles: nothing is permanent, everything returns.

## Language

A tonal language with clicks and harmonics. Written form uses geometric patterns that encode meaning in shape relationships.

## Technology

Masters of solar energy and atmospheric processing. Their ships are elegant, efficient, designed for centuries of operation.
